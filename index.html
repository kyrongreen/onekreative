The Process

Simple as Three Steps

No guesswork. No design skills needed. We handle everything from concept to carrier - you just watch the customers come in.

01

Reserve Your Spot

Choose your ad size and side on the 9×12 mailer. With only 10 slots across both sides - and one per business category - spots go fast.

02

We Design Your Ad

Share your business info, offer, and brand colors. Our team builds a professional, eye-catching ad - included in your placement fee. Two rounds of revisions.

03

It Hits Every Door

USPS delivers your mailer to every home on the route - no list required. Every two weeks, your business is in thousands of hands.

Why It Works

Physical Mail Still Wins

In a world of scrolling, a 9×12 postcard stands out on the kitchen counter. EDDM consistently outperforms digital ads for local service businesses.

  • ✉

    No Targeting Required

    EDDM covers every address on a route - no list, no data, no guesswork. Every household sees your message.

  • ✦

    Premium 9×12 Format

    Larger than a standard mailer, impossible to overlook. Your ad gets prime real estate on the biggest postcard in the mailbox.

  • ↻

    Bi-Weekly Repetition

    Marketing works through repetition. Hitting the same households every two weeks builds brand recognition and trust.

  • $

    Shared Cost = Lower Price

    By co-oping the mailer with other local businesses, your cost drops dramatically while still reaching thousands of homes.

  • 🔒

    Your Category. Your Spot. Period.

    We only place one ad per service type across the entire mailer - front and back. No competing with another plumber, salon, or restaurant. If you're in, you're the only one.

  • ◫

    Front & Back - All Unique

    Both sides of the 9×12 carry ads, but every slot is a different business. 10 unique advertisers, maximum variety, zero repetition.
